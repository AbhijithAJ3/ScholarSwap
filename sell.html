Selling Tips & Safety

  • Be honest about the condition of your item to avoid disputes.
  • Take clear, well-lit photos from multiple angles.
  • Set a fair price - check similar listings for reference.
  • Meet in public places on campus for exchanges.
  • Never share personal financial information.
  • Use ScholarSwap's messaging system for all communications.
